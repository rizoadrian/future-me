How do I export from my bank?
Checking & savings
  • Chase: Account → Download → CSV
  • Bank of America: Accounts → Download → CSV
  • Wells Fargo: Accounts → Download Activity → Comma Delimited File
  • Charles Schwab: Accounts → History → Export
  • Ally: Transactions → Export → CSV
  • US Bank: My Accounts → Download → CSV
Credit cards
  • Chase: Account → Download → CSV
  • Citi: Account Activity → Download → CSV
  • Capital One: View Transactions → Download CSV
  • American Express: Statements → Download CSV
